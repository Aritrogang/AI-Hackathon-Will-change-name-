Six Dimensions of Risk

Duration risk is primary. Weather is the force multiplier.

SVB died from duration mismatch, not a hurricane. The engine scores the structural fragility first, then models what breaks it.

30% Weight · Primary
Duration Risk (WAM)

Weighted Average Maturity of the treasury portfolio. Longer WAM = more fragile. SVB had a 2 year average. Our engine would have flagged it weeks before the run.

OCC filings · LLM extraction · 10-K
20% Weight
Reserve Transparency

Attestations say "US regulated bank with $100B+ in assets." That's an envelope, not data. Our probabilistic entity resolver cross references 10K filings and on chain Mint/Burn flows to infer the actual counterparty (e.g., "BNY Mellon, 92% confidence").

FDIC · Etherscan · 10-K filings
15% Weight
Geographic + Ops Concentration

HHI index of bank locations plus data center corridor overlap. If treasury ops run on AWS us east 1 and a hurricane hits Northern Virginia, redemption processing freezes, even if the bank is fine.

Nominatim · AWS/Azure zones · HHI
15% Weight · Demo Focus
Weather Tail-Risk Multiplier

The hurricane doesn't hit the bank. It hits the bank's local economy. FDIC Call Reports reveal Real Estate Loan Concentration by geography. A Cat 5 in Florida means LTV ratios plummet, triggering a liquidity freeze where the bank can't process withdrawals.

NOAA · NHC · FDIC Call Reports
15% Weight
Counterparty Health

Named banks tracked against FDIC watch lists, LTV ratios, and liquidity coverage. Multi model LLM jury (Claude + Gemini) independently score each bank. If models disagree by >15 points, the signal is flagged for review.

FDIC · LLM consensus jury
5% Weight
Peg Stability

Historical depeg events, current spread, mint/burn velocity. The lagging indicator: by the time the peg moves, the structural damage is already done. Useful for backtest validation.

Architecture

Five layers. Duration risk is the backbone. Weather is the proof.

The architecture separates structural fragility (WAM, concentration) from event catalysts (weather, rate shocks). That's what makes the SVB backtest valid.

01
Ingestion + Entity Resolution

OCC filings and attestation PDFs fed to Claude for extraction. Probabilistic entity resolver cross references bank 10K filings and onchain Mint/Burn flows to identify probable counterparties from vague disclosures.

Knowledge Graph + Data Center Corridors

Stablecoin to Bank (WAM, LTV, liquidity) to City/State to Jurisdiction. Data center corridor nodes map AWS/Azure zones for operational risk. Weather events attach as stress multipliers on bank + DC nodes.

Duration + Stress Engine + MCP Server

Primary: WAM of treasury portfolio (duration mismatch score). Multiplier: weather tail risk × geographic concentration × LTV deterioration. LLM jury (Claude + Gemini) for qualitative consensus scoring. MCP server: 5 tools for AI agent consumption.
